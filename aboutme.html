About Me

Hi, I'm Daniel Banquo

I'm a professional photographer based in Lagos, with a passion for capturing life's most beautiful moments. I specialize in wedding, portrait, and event photography. My unique style blends vivid colors and candid moments, creating timeless images that tell compelling stories.

My journey into photography began 10 years ago when I picked up my first camera. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a lifelong passion. Over the years, I've honed my skills and developed a unique style that blends vivid colors and candid moments.
